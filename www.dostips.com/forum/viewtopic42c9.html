No, I don't plan to redistribute all of the stuff from the Gotoxy archive (http://www.dostips.com/forum/viewtopic.php?f=3&t=7129)

But I thought maybe the solitaire games could deserve their own thread.

Link: http://www.mediafire.com/download/ho7qae0px6qd781/solitaire.zip



Run either games.bat for a menu, or solitaire.bat/freecell.bat/golf.bat individually.

BONUS: In solitaire.bat, press Shift-a to make the "AI" start playing automagically (press ESC to resume manual play).


Other things included that may be interesting: hiscore.bat to easily add hiscores to any game (golf.bat uses it here), menu.bat to create launch menus, cardstack.bat/playcard.bat to help create card games.
