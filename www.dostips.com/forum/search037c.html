SOLVED:
alphaniner in another forum solved it:
for %%F in (*rar,*.zip) do "C:\Program Files\7-Zip\7z.exe" l "%%F" | findstr /m /i /e ".car" >NUL && move "%%F" cars >NUL
The code above worked 100%
