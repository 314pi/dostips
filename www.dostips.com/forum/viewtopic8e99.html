Foxidrive had been suffering severe health problems in recent years and 
unfortunately passed away on Thursday 3rd November.

Though we never met, we did communicate infrequently over many years via 
email, BBS message areas and then newsgroups.

He had very considerable technical knowledge and regularly assisted many 
other members of the computer club to which we both belonged.

On very many occasions he demonstrated his coding skills here and in other 
newsgroups.

He is sadly missed and is now at peace.
